Conventions

Base URL: /api
Authorization: Bearer <token> for all non-public routes
Date format: YYYY-MM-DD
Money: integer kobo

Legal Automation

When a tenant is assigned to an apartment, a tenancy agreement is generated and emailed to the tenant. The agreement includes a clause that a quit notice will be issued automatically if the lease expires without renewal.

When a lease expires without renewal, a quit notice is generated and emailed to the tenant, and the apartment becomes immediately available to the public (status = vacant, is_public = true).
